Opening files

Oct 18, 2022

First of all, a big thank you to the JavaFX community for warmly welcoming us on Twitter. Just a couple of days after our initial tweets, you were already 100+ to follow us! And JFX-Central kindly listed us in their Links of the Week on September 30. Thank you all for your interest in the WebFX project! ❤️

It was also an opportunity for us to receive questions, and the most frequent question was if we could access local files. WebFX was actually not supporting this feature at that time, but we investigated this point, and we are glad to announce that it’s now possible, as demonstrated with our brand-new Files demo.

This demo lets you select local files with either the Choose files button or drag & drop. The files are displayed in a list with an icon and other simple details, and a thumbnail is displayed for images. A grid view is also possible with just the thumbnails. You can open images in full size, and navigate with mouse clicks, left & right keys or swipes on touch screens. Same with audio files, which are played together with the equaliser from our DemoFX library. Video files will be supported very soon (only the audio is played for now). Text files are can be displayed as well in a TextArea.
